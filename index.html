Welcome to the Cat Café !

Cat Coffee is a wonderful convergence of food and cats.
We serve delicious organic treats while offering a place for adoptable cats to relax and play with you.

Within a group of maximum 6 people within the lounge at all time, enjoy our food and play with the cats, who knows, maybe you'll be coming home with a new furry friend!

How does it work?

Cat coffee is both a coffee shop and a cat adoption center. For that purpose, we partner with 3 local shelters

For a $10 fee, customers can stay in the lounge for a full hour. The fee allows us to provide care for the 5 cats waiting for their furrever home! We also take reservations, however remember that cats sleep upwards to 20 hours a day so we encourage you to manage expectations and enjoy the unique experience of simply appreciating our feline friends for all of their unique personalities.

Adoption

All the cats in the café come from local shelters, so if you come by and find yourself attached to one of them, let us know! Adoptions are handled via an application, so don't hesitate to ask our staff!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I share my food with the cats?

No, we cannot guarantee food that is 100% cat-friendly so please, keep an eye on your food and don't let any of the cats get their paws on it!

Does the entry fee cover all food and beverages?

No, the fee is exclusively for the care of the cats. Any food or beverage purchased comes at an additional price

Are childrens allowed?

Yes! Children of all ages are welcome, but keep in mind that this is also a sanctuary for our furry friends, so please keep any noise at a reasonable level.

Can I play with the cats?

Yes! We only ask that you respect the cat's body language: if a cat is sleeping or trying to create distance, respect it and let them come to you. If you have any doubt, don't hesitate to ask the staff for any pointers

Can I bring my dog?

Dogs and any other pet are not allowed within the lounge as we try to create a peaceful and stress-free environment for the cats
